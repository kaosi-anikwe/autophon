Ismail Damilola Raji
Research & Development
<a href="https://github.com/funaquarius24" target="_blank" style="color: currentColor; text-decoration: none; border-bottom: 0.3px dotted #949494;">Ismail Damilola Raji</a> is a freelance software engineer who specializes in AI and machine learning. He joined Autophon in spring 2023 and is responsible for the monophone duplicator that now allows us to train the <a href="https://montreal-forced-aligner.readthedocs.io/en/v1.0/" target="_blank" style="color: currentColor; text-decoration: none; border-bottom: 0.3px dotted #949494;">Montreal Forced Aligner</a> (which is the main component of Autophon's backend) on a reduced and simplified inventory of monophones. Ismail's role is in R&D, and he currently is working on a language-agnostic post-processor that should improve accuracy and be in production by 2024. He received both his M.S. in Computer Engineering (2023) and his B.Eng. in Electrical and Computer Engineering (2015) from Ahmadu Bello University Zaria in Nigeria.
